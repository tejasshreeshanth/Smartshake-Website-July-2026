The Problem

POST-WORKOUT NUTRITION IS BROKEN

Most gyms in India leave their members with nothing after a hard session. The current options are messy, unhygienic, unreliable, or simply unaffordable. We set out to fix that.

  • 01

    No Nutrition Options

    Most gyms offer nothing post-workout. Members are left to fend for themselves.

  • 02

    Messy & Inconvenient

    Carrying tubs, scoops and shakers to the gym is bulky and impractical.

  • 03

    Hygiene Issues

    Shared mixing areas create contamination risks that no one should accept.

  • 04

    Imported = Expensive

    International vending machines are priced out of reach for most Indian gyms.

  • 05

    Existing Domestic Options

    Current Indian vending machines lack quality, reliability, and any meaningful after-sales support.
