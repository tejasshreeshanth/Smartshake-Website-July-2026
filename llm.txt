# Smartshake Vending

> Smartshake Vending LLP is a Chennai-based **manufacturer** of India's first smart protein shake vending machine (SSVM) for gyms and fitness centres. The company **designs, builds, sells, and installs** machines that dispense fresh whey and plant-based protein shakes in under 60 seconds, with cashless UPI payment, a hygienic sealed self-cleaning system, and IoT remote monitoring. Founded in 2025 and backed by an NTU Singapore grant (August 2025), Smartshake is launching June 2026 in Chennai and beyond.

Smartshake is a **hardware manufacturer. The company sells and installs the machines; the gym owner or operator owns and runs the machine day-to-day (stocking powder, setting prices, collecting revenue). Smartshake provides installation, warranty, and optional maintenance support. The machines are compatible with all supplement powders (whey, plant-based, mass gainers, creatine, pre-workout, BCAA, and more).

## Product: SSVM001
The flagship machine, aimed at commercial and large gyms. Key specifications:
- Dispense time: under 60 seconds
- Powder compatibility: all supplement powders (whey, plant-based, mass gainers, creatine, pre-workout, BCAA)
- Flavours: Chocolate, Vanilla, Banana, Strawberry, Coffee, Vegan
- Base options: milk, water, or cold water
- Payment: UPI / QR (all UPI apps)
- Cleaning: automatic clean-in-place (CiP) auto-flush after every use
- Display: 16" touchscreen
- Dimensions: 170 × 70 × 70 cm
- Power: 230V AC / 50Hz
- Flavour slots: 5 independent hoppers
- Cups: disposable
- Connectivity: Wi-Fi, with live remote monitoring dashboard
- Installation: plug & play — Smartshake handles setup and installation
- Warranty: 1 year; free installation, technical support, AMC available

## How it works
1. Pick a flavour on the touchscreen.
2. Select a base (milk, water, or cold water).
3. Pay via any UPI app (contactless).
4. Grab and go — shake ready in under 60 seconds.

## Who it's for
Smartshake sells machines to buyers who want to own and operate them:
- *Gym owners & operators:* purchase a machine to offer members a premium amenity and run it as their own revenue stream, with real-time sales analytics via the remote dashboard.
- *Entrepreneurs & wellness businesses:* buy and place machines in high-footfall locations.

End users (gym members and the public) get affordable, hygienic, freshly made protein on demand, with whey and vegan options and multiple flavours.

Suitable locations include commercial gyms, sports academies, corporate fitness centres, hotel and resort gyms, university campus gyms, CrossFit/functional fitness boxes, and (beyond the gym) corporate offices, cafes and food courts, and public spaces such as metros and airports.
